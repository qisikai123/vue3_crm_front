Terms and Conditions (T&C) of Value Added Service (s)
  1. The Service aims to help protect the Customer’s device(s) against known malwares and phishing sites. Customers need to use the DNS IP provided by the Company. Once a domain or URL is recognized as malware sites or phishing sites by the Company under the Service, any user access attempts to the domain or URL will be restricted. While the Company will use reasonable endeavors to ensure the availability of the Service and prompt detection of all known malwares and phishing sites, the Company does not in any way warrant or guarantee that the Service will be continuous and completely fault-free, nor does it warrant or guarantee in any way that all malwares and phishing sites will be promptly detected and restricted or detected and restricted at all.
  2. Direct access to the IP addresses of the malwares or phishing sites will not be blocked by the Service. Access to the malwares or phishing sites via HTTPS protocols will also not be blocked by the Service.
  3. Web filtering intercepts the HTTP and HTTPS request and checks the categorization of the destination of HKT's web filtering database via Business Netvigator. Either block or permit will be resulted based on the categorization result. Eligible customers are allowed to customize the profiles details via the user portal provided by HKT.
  4. HKT provides this information security service via industry-renowned vendor’s self-contained system, and HKT is not required to monitor the vendor’s operation or details of their website analysis.
  5. HKT does not guarantee all kinds of internet protocol and/or application going through these ports could work/are compatible with the web filtering service and is not responsible for the same. For any protocol or application issue, Customer is responsible for seeking support from its application software vendor for solution.
  6. Google Safe-search is turned on by default by these industry-renowned vendors, however this is not available when user using other search provider or doing HTTPS search. Eligible customers are allowed to turn off or turn on Google Safe-search via the user portal provided by HKT.
  7. For any service change request from existing Customer, the change will be made during office hour within 3 working days.
